From Notes to Knowledge: Tailoring Future Client Interactions

This project aims to boost MFS sales team's efficiency with financial advisors by developing a tool that provides interaction history and recommends conversation topics that would drive sales. By using NLP technology, clustering, and a topic assignment model, it creates a summary document for sales professionals to better prepare for sales client meetings.

Optimizing Airport Arrival Times Using Policy Trees

This project develops a machine learning methodology to forecast flight delays using Optimal Regression Trees and optimize airport arrival schedules with Policy Trees, improving travel planning and reducing waiting times. By combining predictive analytics with personalized decision-making frameworks, it aims to transform how passengers plan for airport arrivals and enhance their overall travel experience.

Context: 15.095 Machine Learning Under a Modern Optimization Lens at MIT

Team members: Mackenzie Lees

Dashboard for Uber's General Manager in NYC

This project involves creating an interactive dashboard analyzing Uber's performance in New York City, comparing it to Lyft and taxis using 2021 TLC Trip Record Data to uncover actionable insights for market optimization. By incorporating filters for time, boroughs, and key metrics, along with visualizations of ride trends, fares, and geographic demand shifts, it equips Uber managers with strategic tools to enhance market presence and operational efficiency.

Context: 15.819 Marketing & Product Analytics at MIT

Team members: Fiona Aga, Maxime Allouch, Vojta Machytka
